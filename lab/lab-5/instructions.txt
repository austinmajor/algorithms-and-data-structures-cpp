CS 400 Lab 5
Implement an infix expression to postfix expression convertor and present your code.

Note:
- Support +, -, *, /, ( and ) in infix expressions.
- Operands will be nonnegative only.
- Assume infix expressions are valid and well formatted (one blank space to separate
operand/operator)

For example,
- 3 * 4 + 5 ➔ 3 4 * 5 +
- 3 + 4 * 5 ➔ 3 4 5 * +
- (3 + 4) * (5 + 6) ➔ 3 4 + 5 6 + *
